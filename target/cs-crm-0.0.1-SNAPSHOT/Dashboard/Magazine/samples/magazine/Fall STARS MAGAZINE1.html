This season is all about fur! From neutral colors to disco and grunge flares, the fur flow is everywhere this fall! We have seen it big last year, this year it’s even BIGGER! Runways were packed with fur, from accessories, bags, shoes to inevitable coats, jackets and vests, worn just during day as much as night. From shaggy street style looks to feminine, luxurious night looks, fur is your wardrobe’s best friend. Wear it in dipped fluorescent paints, bleached in acid, or simply in grey and neutral tones, they are just what you need this season!

 

With endless styles, designs & colors, here are the best tips to pick fur coats wise & wear them even wiser!

The secret to looking great in your fur coat lies mainly in the coat itself. Make sure you pick a perfect fit coat, suitable for your style and figure. Buy your statement, glamorous fur coat to match it with your clothes and accessories to emphasize such a put-together style. Doing the complete opposite, will yet give more edge and sophistication to your look, turning a somewhat classic look into a trendier, more daring one.

• Colors: Coats are seen widely this season in black, white and brown. Animal prints can rest assured too, and so can bold colors including purple, red, pink, neon and prints. Besides, grey which is one of the biggest hit color for this season, don’t miss it!

• Classic Look: For a classic look opt for mid length (around the hips coats). Add a new twist this season with the mid length sleeve, which will allow you to show off your blouse or sweater sleeves.

• Trendy Look: Wear short and waist length coats with a maxi skirt or dress and boots, for a “stay safe”, yet trendy look.

• Day Look: What’s great for any day look this fall, is that anyone can wear a fur coat and pull it off big time, whether in skinny jeans and ankle boots, or in leather leggings, sweater and some grungy motorcycle boots, you will just look fabulous! Not feeling the heels during this cold season? Opt for flat boots, wedges or even some trendy sneakers.

• Night Out Look: Opt for a fur vest or a coat (in medium or long length). Pair it with a sleek dress, cute pumps or ankle boots and a chic clutch and feminine accessories, for a look that is drop dead gorgeous.

How To Bring Tartan Into Your Wardrobe This Fall?

For a casual, yet trendy look, wear your tartan shirt unbuttoned over a tank top with denim jeans and a pair of ankle boots.

For a corporate, fashionable look, button your shirt and place a statement necklace under the collar and team it back with a high waisted black skirt or slim leg black pant, patent leather heel or ballet flat and a versatile carry all tote.

Opt for a statement accessory as an easy way to incorporate tartan to your look if you are not too sure about this bold pattern in an entire garment but still want to give it a try. A tartan scarf is a great idea; it’s both fashionable and functional as you can look on trend and stay warm.

Mix tartan all together! YES! This print on print combination will probably appeal to the more adventurous fashion follower however, definitely worth considering if you daring enough. Wearing head to toe tartan is totally acceptable and can look extremely stylish. When wearing any form of check be it tartan, plaid or houndstooth always treat the pattern as a focal point to your outfit and ensure other colors and styles you wear compliment the colors in the pattern and don’t detract from it.
